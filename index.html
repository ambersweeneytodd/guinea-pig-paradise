            Requirements to Adopt:

      Be at least 21 years of age
      Have a U.S. government-issued photo ID, such as a driver's license
      Verify you are allowed to keep a breed of animal where you reside
      Be able to spend time a resources to care for said animal
      Take all family members to meet the new animal to bond with it before you take it home!
